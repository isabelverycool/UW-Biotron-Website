About Us

our team

The UW Biomechatronics Design Team (aka Biotron) is a design team at the University of Waterloo that designs and builds biomechanical devices to solve biomedical problems. We are a group of science and engineering students that create solutions to emerging and existing problems in the biomedical field.

Our team is comprised of 2 current sub-teams: EMG Fabric and e-NABLE. Each team is working on creating solutions to different biomedical issues that you can learn more about in Projects.
